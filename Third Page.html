VIVI CREATIVE TECHNOLOGY WEB ASSIGNMENT

Concept: the boundary between the virtual and the real is explored by visiting a website that consists of spatio-temporal videos of plants being recorded in different locations while socializing. Purpose: To provoke the viewer to think about whether time and space recorded in memory are virtual? Is the ever-present network information virtual? Is the real world made up of virtual worlds? Are the traces of human visits left in the network virtual? Is history virtual? Have humans finished uploading their consciousness and live in the virtual world forever?
